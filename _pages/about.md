I am an Associate Professor at the LMU Munich, heading the [Munich Uncertainty Quantification AI Lab](https://www.muniq.ai) and a Principal Investigator of the [Munich Center for Machine Learning (MCML)](https://mcml.ai/). 
I&nbsp;am also an Associated Fellow of the [Konrad Zuse School of Excellence in Reliable AI (relAI)](https://zuseschoolrelai.de/) and an [Ellis Member](https://ellis.eu/). 

My current research involves the development of **uncertainty quantification** for deep learning approaches (mostly [Bayesian](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v235/papamarkou24b.html)) and studying [**overparametrization** in neural networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.03571). See also my [Google Scholar profile](https://scholar.google.de/citations?hl=de&user=_DYguksAAAAJ&view_op=list_works&sortby=pubdate) for some of my recent research.

I am committed to advancing my research field through active participation in the academic community: 
* as an action editor for *JSS*&nbsp;&nbsp; and *TMLR*,
* as a program chair for *NeurIPS* (Position Paper Track) and [*ProbML*](http://www.probml.cc/),
* as (senior) area chair for several conferences such as *AIStats*, *ICLR*, *ICML*, *NeurIPS*, and *UAI*,
* by participating in strategic meetings such as [Dagstuhl](https://www.dagstuhl.de/en/seminars/seminar-calendar/seminar-details/24461),
* by supporting early-career researchers through exchange visits of my students, for example to Emmanuel Candès' lab at Stanford or Emtiyaz Khan's lab in Tokyo, and by hosting visitors in Munich.

For more information, visit my [group's homepage](https://www.muniq.ai), where you can find information about the awesome people I work with, funders, teaching, and recent news.
